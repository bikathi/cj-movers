Who are we?

CjMovers is the leading house and office moving solutions in Kenya. We specialize in, but not limited to:

  • Full house and office moving, including packing and unpacking of your property, whether close-by or long-distance.
  • TV and wall painting mounting in a sleek and fashionable manner.
  • DSTV installation and improvements to an existing installation including changing of dish mount point.

We also provide WiFi extension and electrical repair and installation services at your home or your office. Have a question about any of these? Don't hesitate, reach out and let us handle the rest.
